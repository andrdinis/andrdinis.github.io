André da Cunha Dinis

Digital Product Designer

My work as a creative is not about the medium, but about the impact. Irreversibly curious and passionate about the human, the art and the technology.

My artistic education started at the António Arroio School of Arts with Industrial Design, where I was introduced to design thinking, product concept and accessibility, through ergonomics. I then enrolled in a bachelor degree at the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Lisbon, where I studied Multimedia Art and Identity Design — deepening technical skills in areas such as interaction design.

My academic experience converged into Digital Experience Design, which I studied in my post-graduation. Working in the different areas of art, technology and design has provided me with distinct skills that have shaped the way I think and create.
