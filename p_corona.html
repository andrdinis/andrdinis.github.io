Dismorfia Comunitária

Photo Collection · 2020

"Are we singular without experiences that dynamize us individually? Are we be plural in community dysmorphia?"

A visual essay on the period of social isolation. Depersonalization syndrome, or de-realization, is a symptom of mental disorder that causes a break with reality. The project addresses a reflection on the disorder that social isolation causes singular and plurally in humans - a break with reality, a non-pathological depersonalization. Two contemporary realities are reflected — the virtual and the mother reality - creating a contrast ensured by expectations and companionship.
